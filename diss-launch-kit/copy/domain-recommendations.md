- **Why:** Memorable, action-oriented, slightly provocative. "Get $DISS'd" is a natural call-to-action that works in tweets, memes, and marketing.

- **Brand fit:** 9/10

### 3. dissensus.ai

- **Brand fit:** 9/10 (if available)
